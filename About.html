ABOUT SAN FELIPE NERI PAROCHIAL SCHOOL

San Felipe Neri Parochial School is a Good School located at the Tiger City of the Philippines known as Mandaluyong City. It offers excellent education at the same time molding their students to become humble and God Fearing person at all times. The school is considered as a home wherein the students were being nurtured with love and discipline. It's main task not only enhancing their student's knowledge,but also helping them to improve their skills,talents and personality as well.

Brief History of San Felipe Neri Parochial School

San Felipe Neri Parochial School started by Miss Rosa A. Salazar who was then acting as a President of the Parish Confraternity of our Lady of Lourdes in 1965.

A teacher by profession, Miss Rosa A. Salazar began using the backyard of the San Felipe Neri Parish for classroom activities. The school operated as a nursery center for the next two years. In June 1965, the school opened as a full time educational institution with a permitto operate from the former Department of Education. Read More
